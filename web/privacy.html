Privacy Policy

Effective: June 24, 2026 · Version 2026-06-22

Mangasm (“we”, “us”) operates the Mangasm iOS application and this website. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Account data: sign-in identifier (e.g. Apple ID token), display name, age affirmation timestamp.
  • Profile data you provide: photos, bio, interests, optional sensitive fields (hidden by default until you opt in).
  • Usage & safety: reports, blocks, consent logs, reputation signals.
  • Purchases: subscription status via Apple In-App Purchase (we do not store card numbers).
  • Location: coarse neighbourhood-level zones only — we do not display raw precise coordinates to other members.

What we do not do

  • Sell personal data to data brokers.
  • Run third-party advertising networks inside the app.
  • Collect health data (HIV status was removed from the product entirely).

Legal basis & retention

We process data to provide the service, ensure safety, and comply with law. You may delete your account in-app; deletion removes your profile and cascades associated data except where retention is required by law.
