Moderation Policy

Required for user-generated content apps · Updated June 24, 2026

Reporting

Members can report profiles, messages, and events from the in-app overflow (⋯) menu. Categories include harassment, spam, fake profiles, underage concern, and other.

Response time

We review reports within 24 hours. Severe safety issues (underage, threats, non-consensual content) are prioritised for immediate action.

Enforcement actions

  • Content removal
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent ban
  • Referral to law enforcement when required

Blocking

Blocking is bidirectional — blocked members cannot see each other’s profiles or messages.

Appeals

Email support@mangasm.app with your username and report reference within 14 days of an enforcement action.
