## VERSION A: 60–90 Second Teaser

**Working title:** *Where the Signal Shouldn't Reach*
**Tone:** Fashion runway. Deep house. Invitation withheld, then extended.
**Target runtime:** 75 seconds

---

### Music Cue

**Entrance beat:** Playa deep-house — think Guy Gerber, Bedouin, or Colyn. Starts at 60–70 bpm, builds slowly. No drop until the final 10 seconds. Bass is felt before it is heard. A steady four-on-the-floor pulse under something hypnotic. The music does not perform — it occupies the room.

Key moment: at 0:35 (the wordmark reveal), the kick drum locks in. At 1:05, a subtle filter sweep lifts — not a drop, a breath.

---

### Shot-by-Shot

---

**[0:00–0:04]**
**Visual:** Black. Absolute. Then, at the centre of frame, a single point of gold light — barely a pixel — begins to breathe. Slow pulse. Like a candle far away.
**VO:** (None.)
**On-screen text:** (None.)
**Music:** Sub-bass hum. Pre-beat.

---

**Visual:** Cut hard to: the runway. Slow-motion. A model — angular, striking — walks toward camera from depth. Steampunk-luxury fashion. The catwalk is obsidian-black. A sophisticated audience lines both sides, blur. The atmosphere is dark, cinema-grade, orange-haze overlay.
**VO:** (None.)
**On-screen text:** `EST. MMXXVI` — appears bottom-left. Cormorant Garamond, tracked out, gold. Letter by letter.
**Music:** The kick drum enters. Deliberate. Measured.

**[0:10–0:18]**
**Visual:** Series of cuts, each 2 seconds. Same runway, different framing — extreme close-up of a hand, then a collar, then a profile. Then wide: the audience in ornate gowns, the digital rain of the cyberpunk backdrop cascading behind. A forearm glimpsed: `MANGASM` tattooed in serif.
**VO:** (None — music only through this section.)
**On-screen text:** (Nothing new. Let the images hold.)
**Music:** A second melodic element enters — a high string or synth pad. Feels expensive.

**[0:18–0:28]**
**Visual:** Fast cuts to city nightscapes — Dubai from above (gold grid, infinite scale), London in rain (wet streets reflecting neon), Mykonos at dusk (white walls, impossible light), Tokyo at night (velvet and neon). Each city: 2–3 seconds. Push-in. Not travelogue — aspiration.

**VO:** *"Access is earned. Brotherhood is the currency."*
**On-screen text:** None.
**Music:** The kick is confident now. Almost there.

**[0:38–0:50]**
**Visual:** App interface glimpses — not a product demo, a mood. The gold compatibility ring animating. A message thread, encrypted. An event card reading `By invitation · Members only`. The AI Match screen, the wordmark Mangasm glowing gold. Each shot: 2 seconds, partial reveal.

`E2E Encrypted`
`Reputation-gated`

**[0:50–1:02]**
**Visual:** Slow fade — the runway goes dark. A single frame: the wordmark `Mangasm` in Cormorant Garamond, white, on black. Then the gold glow loop begins — the wordmark pulses, breathes. Below it, in tracked mono: `DARK LUXURY · BROTHERHOOD · NIGHTLIFE`.

**On-screen text:** `DARK LUXURY · BROTHERHOOD · NIGHTLIFE`

**VO:** (None — let the visual speak.)

**[1:12–END]**
**Visual:** Black. The gold pixel — the same one from the opening — returns. Breathes once. Fades.
**On-screen text:** (None.)
**Music:** Last sustained note. Silence.

## VERSION B: 2–3 Minute Founder / Explainer

**Working title:** *Why We Built Mangasm*
**Tone:** Calm authority. Personal. The founder in a dark, well-lit room — honest, not performative.
**Target runtime:** 2 min 45 sec

---

### Music Cue

Same deep-house bed, lower in the mix. Barely audible under the voice. Fades to near-silence during the most personal moments, returns during transitions.

---

### Script

---

**[0:00–0:15]**
**Visual:** Dark room. Founder facing camera, medium close-up. Gold light from below. No product branding yet.
**VO / Spoken:**
*"Gay men built the internet's first communities. We built the underground networks, the invitation lists, the safest rooms we could find. And then we handed those rooms to platforms that treated us like inventory."*

---

**[0:15–0:35]**
**Visual:** Cut to app glimpses — the sign-in screen, the city slides (Dubai, London, Mykonos, Tokyo), the invitation sheet.
**VO / Spoken:**
*"Mangasm started with one question: what would a gay dating app look like if it was built by gay men who actually gave a damn about safety? Not safety as a disclaimer — safety as the architecture. Reputation before a photo. Trust before a match."*

---

**[0:35–0:55]**
**Visual:** Profile screen — the vouch system, the reputation ring, the reputation-gated photos grid.
**VO / Spoken:**
*"Every member has a reputation score. Built by vouches — real men who know you and are willing to say so publicly. Your photos stay private until you choose to open them. Your location is a zone, not a pin. You control what exists about you in this community."*

---

**[0:55–1:15]**
**Visual:** AI Match screen — the compatibility ring animating, the astrology/numerology breakdown rows.
**VO / Spoken:**
*"The matching works differently here. We believe that compatibility isn't surface-level. So we built AI scoring that factors in astrology, numerology, and zodiac alignment — alongside the things you'd expect. The result is matches that feel considered. Men who are actually aligned with who you are."*

---

**[1:15–1:35]**
**Visual:** Chat thread — E2E banner, the gold bubbles, the encrypted interface.
**VO / Spoken:**
*"Every conversation is end-to-end encrypted. What you say here does not exist anywhere else. No data brokering. No ads. No algorithm that sells your attention. You are not the product."*

---

**[1:35–1:55]**
**Visual:** Events screen — event cards, the consent note, the host form, the community tabs.
**VO / Spoken:**
*"Events on Mangasm are different too. Every event has a consent framework built in — 18+, ID at the door, no recording. Hosted by members who have earned the right to host. This is not a party notification. It is a curated gathering with accountability behind it."*

---

**[1:55–2:15]**
**Visual:** Return to founder. Slight push-in. More personal.
**VO / Spoken:**
*"I'm not going to pretend this is for everyone. It isn't. Access is earned through invitation and reputation. We are not trying to be the biggest room. We are trying to be the right room."*

---

**[2:15–2:35]**
**Visual:** Splash screen — runway, wordmark, the gold glow loop.
**VO / Spoken:**
*"Mangasm is launching by invitation. If you're watching this — someone in the community thought you belonged here. We think so too."*

---

**[2:35–2:45]**
**Visual:** Wordmark holds. CTA appears.
**VO / Spoken:**
*"Join the waitlist. Or ask someone inside to vouch for you. Either way — we'll see you in the room."*
**On-screen text:** `mangasm.app` · `ENTER THE COMMUNITY →`
